FAQ

01. Is there a fee to post a project?

It is free to post a project no fees required

02. How do I find freelancers for my project?

Posting a project on Cloudtask will get your project in front of the most qualified freelancers and agencies by bids. You will then get bids for the project with the applicant’s details and reasons why they are the best fit for the projects. You can also search for freelancers and invite them to apply.

03. How do I pay freelancers & agencies?

You’re free to pay your freelancer and agencies. you can pay automatically for their work through Paypal, Payoneer, or (which allows you to pay via credit card, debit card).
